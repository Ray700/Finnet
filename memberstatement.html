    • How to View Member Statement

    Step 1: Log In

    1. Visit our portal website

    2. Enter your member ID

    3. Input your password

    4. Click "Sign In"

    Step 2: Access Account Dashboard

    1. Navigate to the main menu

    2. Click "My Account" option

    3. Select "Account Overview"

    Step 3: Generate Statement

    1. Choose statement type

    2. Select date range

    3. Click "Generate Report"

    4. Download PDF
