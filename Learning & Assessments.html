Learning & Assessments Guide

How do I watch course videos?

1. Navigate to E.

2. Click "Play" on the video player.

3. Use controls to pause, rewind, or adjust volume.

Example of the video player interface.

How do I attempt quizzes and assignments?

1. Go to the course module.

2. Locate the quiz or assignment section.

3. Read instructions and start the quiz/assignment.

Example of attempting a quiz or assignment.

How do I check my quiz or assignment scores?

1. After completing a quiz or assignment, click "View Results."

2. Your score will be displayed along with correct answers.

Example of viewing quiz results.

How do I retake a failed quiz?

1. If allowed, go back to the quiz section.

2. Click "Retake Quiz" and follow the instructions.

3. Review your previous answers for improvement.

Example of retaking a quiz.

How do I participate in live classes or webinars?

1. Check the schedule for upcoming live sessions.

2. Click the "Join Live Class" link at the scheduled time.

3. Follow prompts to join via Zoom or another platform.

Example of joining a live class.

How do I submit an assignment?

1. Navigate to the assignment section within the course.

2. Upload your completed file in the designated area.

3. Confirm submission and wait for grading.

Example of submitting an assignment.
