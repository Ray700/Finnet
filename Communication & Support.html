Communication & Support Guide

How do I interact with instructors?

1. Navigate to the course module or live session.

2. Use the "Ask Instructor" feature or comment section.

3. Instructors will respond within 24 hours.

Example of interacting with instructors.

How do I join a discussion forum?

1. Go to the "Community" or "Discussion Forum" tab in your course.

2. Click "Join Discussion" to participate in ongoing threads.

3. Post questions, replies, or start a new topic.

Example of joining a discussion forum.

How do I message my classmates?

1. Open the "Classmates" or "Participants" list in your course.

2. Select a classmate and click "Send Message."

3. Compose your message and send it.

Example of messaging classmates.

How do I report an issue or ask for support?

1. Navigate to the "Help Center" or "Support" section.

2. Choose the type of issue you're facing.

3. Fill out the support form with details.

4. Submit your request, and our team will assist you shortly.

Example of submitting a support request.
