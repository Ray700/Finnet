How to View News & Events

How do I view news and events?

1. Navigate to the "News & Updates" section in your dashboard.

2. Browse through the latest announcements, articles, or event listings.

3. Click on any announcement or event for more details.

Example of the News & Updates section.

How do I filter news and events?

1. Use the filters available in the "News & Updates" section.

2. Select categories such as "Financial Tips," "Upcoming Webinars," or "Company Announcements."

3. Apply filters to refine the displayed content.

Example of filtering news and events.

How do I subscribe to notifications?

1. Go to "Settings" or "Preferences" in your account.

2. Enable email or push notifications for news and events.

3. Save your preferences to stay updated.

Example of subscribing to notifications.

How do I share an announcement?

1. Open the announcement or event you want to share.

2. Locate the "Share" button or icon.

3. Choose your preferred sharing method (email, social media, etc.).

Example of sharing an announcement.
