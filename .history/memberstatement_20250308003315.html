How to View Member Statement

Step 1: Navigate to Account Section

1. Log into your Pension Portal account

2. Click the "My Account" option from the main menu

Step 2: Access Statement Options

3. Select "View Statement" from the account dashboard

4. Choose statement type (Monthly/Quarterly/Annual)

Step 3: Select Period & Download

5. Use the date picker to select period range

6. Click "Generate Statement" to view/download PDF
