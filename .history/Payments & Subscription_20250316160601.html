Payments & Subscription Guide

How do I pay for a course?

1. Navigate to the course page.

2. Click "Enroll Now" or "Add to Cart."

3. Proceed to checkout and select your payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).

4. Complete the payment process.

Example of the payment process screen.

How do I cancel or upgrade my subscription?

1. Log in to your account.

2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Subscription."

3. To cancel, click "Cancel Subscription." To upgrade, select a new plan and proceed with payment.

Example of the subscription settings page.

How do I apply a discount code?

1. During checkout, locate the "Apply Discount" field.

2. Enter your discount code and click "Apply."

3. The discounted price will be reflected in your total.

Example of applying a discount code.

How do I get a refund?

1. Contact our support team via the "Help Center" or email.

2. Provide your order details and reason for requesting a refund.

3. Our team will review and process your request within 3-5 business days.

Example of submitting a refund request.
